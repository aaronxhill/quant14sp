Corporation X is facing claims of gender discrimination, with accusations that women have lower salaries than men. The Corporation responded by claiming that after considering education and job type, there are no differences in earnings between men and women. 1.	What is the average salary for men at this Corporation? What is the average salary for women? Comment on your findings. 2.	What are the education differences between men and women at this corporation? Comment on your findings. 3.	Using multiple linear regression, determine the current salary difference between men and women, controlling for education, type of job, age, race, time in current job, previous experience, and starting salary. 4.	Write the multiple regression equation. Report the F statistic, R2, and the P-value. 5.	Why didn't we just conduct a t-test to address this research question? Is multiple regression better or worse for this analysis? How so? 6.	Write your findings in one or two paragraphs. Report whether the model was statistically significant; report which of the independent variables are statistically significant. Interpret the multiple regression equation. Summarize your conclusions. Elaborate on what these findings say about the claims of gender pay discrimination.
